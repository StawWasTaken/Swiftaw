Where we're headed

Five goals we're working toward,
out loud.

We publish our goals so we can be held to them. Each one has a plan, a team, and a public commitment.

01

A complete, connected ecosystem by 2027.

Fortized, ID, Cloud and Build — each a first-class product on its own, and all of them tightly integrated for the people using more than one.

ProductRoadmap
02

Document everything we ship.

Every feature, every config flag, every trade-off — written up in plain language before it reaches general availability. No "read the source" as a doc.

DocsCommunication
03

Safety reviews on every public release.

No release crosses into GA without a Safety signoff — even internal tooling. We publish what we looked at and what we found.

SafetyProcess
04

Contribute back to the open stack.

We stand on a pile of open-source work. Our R&D department is measured in part by what we upstream to it.

R&DOpen source
05

Stay small enough to care.

Growth for its own sake corrupts product decisions. We grow the team only as fast as we can keep meeting each other in the same room.

CultureIndependence
