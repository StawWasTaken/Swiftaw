About Swiftaw

We build what we'd want
to use ourselves.

Swiftaw is an independent, research-led technology company assembling one connected ecosystem — product by product, principle by principle.

Our story

Founded to make technology worth trusting.

Swiftaw started from a frustration with how much modern software is assembled from pieces nobody fully understands. We believe the antidote is doing the hard parts ourselves — in the open, with a small team that cares.

We started with one product, Fortized, and a plan: to build a whole ecosystem the same way — secure by default, honest in its docs, and designed to be someone's favorite tool.

In a sentence

Swiftaw is the smallest independent company trying to build the most trustworthy software ecosystem.

What we believe

Principles that actually shape the work

◆

Ship the truth

We don't pretend something is done when it isn't. Our changelogs, docs and betas say exactly where we are.

◇

Design together

R&D, Safety and Communication review every release as equals. Nothing ships without all three.

△

Independent, on purpose

No outside investors steering the roadmap. We build what our users actually need.

○

One ecosystem

Every product we build plugs into the same identity, storage and security fabric — and will stay that way.
