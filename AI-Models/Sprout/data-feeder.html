How Gardener Works

1
Pick a Topic
Each cycle, Gardener picks a topic (science, history, philosophy, etc.) from its rotation list.
2
Ask Gemini
Sends the topic to Google Gemini, which generates real Q&A pairs or writing samples using internet knowledge.
3
Parse & Deduplicate
Parses Gemini's JSON response and checks against existing database entries to avoid duplicates.
4
Feed Database
Inserts new data into Supabase. All Tithonia AI models (Sprout, etc.) read from this shared database.
