our mission, in plain words

tech for the people inside it.

We make things for communities, players, friend groups and weirdos online. Not for metrics, not for noise, not for the graph.

Swiftaw exists to make tech feel inspiring, elegant and purposeful - again. - the whole reason

A tiny European studio. Fortized was the first thing we shipped - a healthy community platform for players. Whatever comes next will follow the same compass: human-first, generously designed, made to last.

We borrow good ideas where we find them. Lately, that's been Pinterest's Gestalt - soft, generous, opinionated about clarity. You'll feel traces of it in what we ship next.
