stampled

some ideas deserve more than a like button.

here's the thing. we, at Swiftaw, are building a new app. and we're not going to be quiet about it.

stampled is a brand-new social app made for the people who are quietly fed up with the apps already sitting on their phone. it's european, it's private by default, and it does more in one place than most of the apps you currently have to juggle between.

we think it's going to make you actually happy to open it. we think it's going to be the first thing you tap in the morning and the last thing you close at night, ahead of every other app you keep around mostly out of habit.

we're not telling you everything yet. you'll see it when it's ready. but here's the rough shape of it -

what we will say
  • social, but not draining. built around small rooms, not infinite feeds.
  • expressive, the way the early internet was, before everything became a grid of squares.
  • safe by default. european hosting, no creepy data deals, no dark patterns.
  • about ten features you've been quietly wishing one single app would just do for you.
  • free. no monthly subscription holding good features hostage.
still early. still weird. still changing every week.
that's usually a good sign.
