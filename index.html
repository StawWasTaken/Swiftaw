01 - About

Independent. Research-driven. European.

At Swiftaw, we believe progress is powered by connection. We don't just ship software - we invest in understanding how it's built, how people use it, and how it can keep improving.

Our R&D arm pursues better underlying technology - faster, smarter interfaces that adapt to people rather than demanding adaptation from them.

Founded in 2025 and headquartered in Ile-de-France, we are a small, independent team operating with a long-term vision.

02 - How we work

Structure, people, teams.

How Swiftaw is organised. Swiftaw runs three centralized departments - R&D, Communication, and Safety - each headed by a chief from leadership. Chiefs operate across all Swiftaw projects. Department personnel are assigned to a specific project team. Teams are project-focused groups that draw from department staff, plus project-specific roles like moderators, admins, and developers.
Departments
R&D Department Tyrianum
Drives research and innovation across all Swiftaw projects. Focused on building better underlying technology - faster systems, smarter interfaces, and adaptive software.
Communication Department Weaverdant
Manages how Swiftaw and its projects present themselves - announcements, public-facing content, and community relations across all platforms.
Safety Department Vacant
Responsible for maintaining safe environments across all Swiftaw projects. Covers moderation policy, user safety standards, and enforcement.
Teams
Active Team Fortized
The project team behind Fortized (Project-S4). Made up of Swiftaw personnel assigned from the three departments - including developers, moderators, and admins - working together on Swiftaw's flagship product.

Department chiefs contribute to Team Fortized in an advisory and oversight capacity, alongside their cross-project responsibilities.
