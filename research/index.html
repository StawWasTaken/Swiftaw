Research

Our research teams explore how to build better AI systems and better social platforms — empowering people through chatting, interacting, connecting, and intelligent assistance.

Research teams: AI Development Social Platforms Human Connection Intelligent Assistance

Better AI

Building smarter, more helpful AI systems

We research how to create AI that truly understands and assists people — from natural conversation to complex problem-solving. Our goal is AI that feels intuitive, reliable, and genuinely useful in everyday life.

Conversational AI

Developing AI that understands context, tone, and intent — enabling natural conversations that help people get things done, learn, and create.

Adaptive Learning Models

AI systems that continuously improve from interactions, becoming more personalized and effective at providing assistance tailored to each user's needs.

Safe & Responsible AI

Researching methods to ensure our AI systems are trustworthy, transparent, and aligned with user well-being — so people can rely on them with confidence.

Social Platforms

Reimagining how people connect online

We research how to build social platforms that bring people closer together — making it easier to chat, share, and form meaningful connections in digital spaces.

Real-Time Chatting

Exploring new ways to make messaging faster, richer, and more expressive — so conversations online feel as natural as talking face to face.

Community Interaction

Studying how people interact in digital communities to design features that encourage healthy engagement, collaboration, and shared experiences.

Meaningful Connections

Researching how platforms can help people discover and build genuine relationships — connecting individuals with shared interests, goals, and passions.

AI Assistance

Empowering people through intelligent assistance

We research how AI can serve as a helpful companion in everyday life — assisting with tasks, answering questions, and supporting people across work, learning, and creativity.

Personal AI Assistants

Developing AI assistants that understand your context and preferences, helping you accomplish tasks more efficiently — from scheduling to problem-solving.

AI-Powered Social Features

Researching how AI can enhance social interactions — from smart suggestions and content discovery to helping people find communities that match their interests.

Accessible Intelligence

Making AI assistance available to everyone — researching how to bring helpful, easy-to-use AI tools to people regardless of their technical background.

   We believe the best technology helps people connect, create, and thrive. Our research is driven by one question: how can better AI and better platforms make life easier and more meaningful for everyone?
