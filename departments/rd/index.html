How we work

Principles in the R&D room

△

Prototype before plan

We build the smallest version first and let it tell us what the real problem is.

◇

Review with Safety

Every non-trivial design gets reviewed with the Safety department before it turns into code.

○

Write down the why

Every merged change carries a short design note. Future-us will need it more than we think.
