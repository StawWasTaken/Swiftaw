Department 03

Communication

Changelogs, incident reports, docs, announcements, support replies — if it has a Swiftaw name on it and faces outward, Communication edits it.

House voice

How we write

Do Name the thing directly

"We broke login for 12 minutes" — not "users may have experienced an authentication-adjacent service quality event."

Don't Hide bad news in passive voice

If something went wrong, somebody did something. Say what.

Do Explain what it costs the reader

"This will log you out on every device" up front, not buried under the heading.

Don't Use marketing adjectives

"Powerful", "next-generation", "revolutionary" — cut them. The thing either is or isn't.

Do Link to the primary source

If it's in the changelog, link the changelog. If it's in a paper, link the paper. Readers deserve the original.

Don't Pretend uncertainty is resolved

"We don't know yet" is almost always the honest answer — and it's almost always the better one.
