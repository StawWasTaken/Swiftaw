Safety

The Safety department protects every user, every community, and every interaction across the Swiftaw ecosystem. We build the systems that make trust possible at scale.

Our focus
AutoMod Raid Protection Data Privacy Trust & Transparency

How we protect.

Safety isn't a feature bolt-on. It's woven into the architecture of everything we ship. From automated moderation to privacy-first design, every layer is built with protection in mind.

🛡

AutoMod Engine

Real-time content filtering powered by machine learning. Pattern detection, context-aware analysis, and configurable sensitivity levels that adapt to each community.

💪

Raid Protection

Automated detection and mitigation of coordinated attacks. Rate limiting, CAPTCHA gates, verification levels, and instant lockdown capabilities for community admins.

🔒

Data Privacy

End-to-end encryption for direct messages. Minimal data collection, transparent retention policies, and full GDPR compliance. Your data stays yours.

👁

Trust & Transparency

Public safety reports, clear community guidelines, and an open appeals process. Every moderation action is logged and reviewable.

✅

Age Verification

Multi-layered age gating that goes beyond simple checkboxes. Identity verification options, age-restricted content channels, and parental controls.

📜

Audit Logging

Comprehensive activity logs for community administrators. Track moderation actions, permission changes, and member activity with granular filtering.

Safety by the numbers.

Our systems work around the clock to keep the ecosystem safe.

Real-Time Scanning

Every message, image, and link is analyzed in under 50ms. Zero perceptible delay for users, maximum protection for communities.

Adaptive Thresholds

AutoMod learns from each community's culture and norms. What's appropriate in a gaming server differs from a study group, and our systems understand that.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We can protect without seeing. Privacy-preserving hash matching for known harmful content means we never need to store or view sensitive material.

Human Review Pipeline

AI catches the volume. Humans handle the nuance. Every escalated case is reviewed by trained safety specialists within 24 hours.
